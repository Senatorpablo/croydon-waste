Our Croydon commitment

Six promises. One borough.

We've built our entire business around the Council's six Social Value themes. Not because we have to — because Croydon raised us, and we believe in giving back to the place we call home.

SV1 — Jobs & Skills

Local jobs for local people.

Every new contract creates apprenticeships and full-time roles recruited exclusively through Croydon Works. We pay the Real Living Wage from day one — no exceptions.

SV2 — Growth

Croydon SMEs in our supply chain.

From the garage that services our vehicles to the printer for our paperwork — we buy local first. Over 80% of our suppliers are Croydon SMEs.

SV3 — Health & Wellbeing

Mental health first. Always.

Mental Health First Aid trained team. Free counselling for staff. We sponsor Croydon community wellbeing events year-round.

SV4 — Equality

Reflecting Croydon's diversity.

Our team mirrors our borough. We actively recruit from groups underrepresented in the waste industry, including people rebuilding their lives after challenges.

SV5 — Stronger Communities

We give back. Visibly.

Free fly-tip clearance for community groups, schools and TRAs. We've cleared over 40 sites for free since launching — and we're just getting started.

SV6 — Cleaner & Greener

Zero to landfill. Net zero by 2030.

94% of waste diverted from landfill today. We're investing in electric tippers and HVO biofuel to hit net zero ahead of Croydon's own 2030 target.
